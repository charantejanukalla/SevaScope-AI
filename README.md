# 🌍 SevaScope AI

**SevaScope AI** is a data-driven platform built to empower NGOs with smarter, faster, and more effective decision-making.

Many NGOs collect large volumes of community data through surveys, reports, field visits, and outreach programs. However, this data is often **unstructured, scattered, and difficult to analyze**, leading to delays and inefficient resource allocation.

SevaScope AI solves this challenge by transforming raw data into **clear, visual, and actionable intelligence** using Artificial Intelligence.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

NGOs frequently face issues such as:

- Community data stored in reports, PDFs, CSVs, and notes
- Difficulty identifying urgent problem areas
- No clear visibility of trends and severity levels
- Slow manual analysis process
- Misallocation of funds, volunteers, and resources

Even when resources are available, lack of insight prevents optimal action.

---

## 💡 Solution

SevaScope AI uses **Google Gemini AI** to process unstructured data and convert it into structured insights.

The platform can:

✅ Analyze reports, surveys, and uploaded files  
✅ Automatically classify social issues  
✅ Assign severity levels (Low / Medium / High / Critical)  
✅ Visualize affected areas using interactive heat maps  
✅ Generate recommendations for NGOs  
✅ Support faster, smarter decision-making

---

## 🧠 AI Features

### 🔹 Smart Data Classification
Identifies issues such as:

- Hunger
- Education gaps
- Health concerns
- Water scarcity
- Sanitation problems
- Employment issues
- Women & child welfare concerns

### 🔹 Severity Detection

Each issue is ranked based on urgency:

- 🟢 Low  
- 🟡 Medium  
- 🟠 High  
- 🔴 Critical

### 🔹 AI Recommendations

Example:

> "Area A has rising water scarcity reports. Immediate tanker support and water camp recommended."

---

## 🗺️ Heat Zone Mapping

SevaScope AI displays community issues on an interactive map.

### Visual Zones:

- 🔴 Red Zone → High Priority
- 🟠 Orange Zone → Medium Priority
- 🟢 Green Zone → Stable Area

This helps NGOs instantly understand **where help is needed most**.
